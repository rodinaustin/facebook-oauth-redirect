Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our application, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the application using your Facebook account.
  2. Navigate to the settings page.
  3. Click on the "Delete My Data" button.
  4. Confirm the deletion by following the on-screen instructions.

If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact us at rodney@us.ibm.com.
